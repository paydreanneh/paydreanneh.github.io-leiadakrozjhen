Behind the camera

Hello, I'm
Paydreanne.

Also known as "Leiada Krözjhen" — I'm an automotive photographer based in Charlotte, NC. I've been shooting since 2018, capturing street portraits, architecture, community events, and more.

After landing a real-estate photography job in 2024, I quickly found myself dissatisfied with the routine of it all. Meanwhile I was deep in Forza Horizon 5, obsessing over the graphics and unique looks of different cars.

"What if I tried out car photography?"

In 2025, I made the switch — collaborating with my local Porsche and Genesis dealerships, and continuing to push the limits of what automotive imagery can look like.
